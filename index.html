FirePatrolAgent

When real-time surveillance images are transmitted to the agent, FirePatrolAgent automatically invokes RobustFireDet(named HLRA-YOLOv9 in the demo) for initial fire detection. If fire targets are identified, and subject to user authorization, the agent subsequently calls RobustFireNet(named HLRA-EfficientNetV2-S in the demo) to perform batch secondary verification on the suspected fire regions. After successful secondary verification, FirePatrolAgent automatically conducts scene analysis based on the detection results. It then utilizes a RAG framework that integrates online search, a local text knowledge base, and a knowledge graph to generate tailored response suggestions for both on-site personnel and management staff.
